All About Go Green Solutions Kenya

Mission Statement

Go Green Solutions is an organisation that is fully dedicated to developing and empowering disadvantaged communities, especially rural families through grassroots action, research, and education. The status of the local natural environment is key in development process. Since the poor and marginalized lack the resources needed to reduce the negative effects of a degraded environment, it affects them the hardest. In the same breath, individuals living with scarce resources are usually dependent on the close environment for thier daily survival.

Team Story

Go Green Solutions was founded by a group of passionate environmental enthusiasts who shared a common goal of preserving and protecting the environment. With years of experience in the industry, the team came together to create a unique platform that empowers individuals to make a positive impact on the environment. Together, they have built a supportive community that is dedicated to promoting sustainable living and environmental conservation.
